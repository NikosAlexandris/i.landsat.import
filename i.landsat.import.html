DESCRIPTION

i.landsat.import is a GRASS-GIS module that imports Landsat satellite imagery in GRASS GIS data base. It treats Landsat data of both forms, tar.gz (packed and compressed) as well as (decompressed and unpacked) GeoTiFF files of a single scene that reside inside an independent directory. The module handles also multiple scenes. It imports all bands that pertain to a scene in one indepenendet Mapset. Date (year, month, day) and time (hours, minutes, seconds, timezone) of acquisition are transferred to each imported band.

Overview

Band 1 - Ultra Blue (coastal/aerosol)
Band 2 - Blue
Band 3 - Green
Band 4 - Red
Band 5 - Near Infrared (NIR)
Band 6 - Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) 1
Band 7 - Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) 2
Band 8 - Panchromatic
Band 9 - Cirrus
Band 10 - Thermal Infrared (TIRS) 1
Band 11 - Thermal Infrared (TIRS) 2
